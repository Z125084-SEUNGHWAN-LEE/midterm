WORKS

Guitar Workshop 3D Modeling

This project is a 3D model of a guitar workshop, designed to capture the atmosphere of a handcrafted instrument studio. The goal was to create a realistic and detailed space that reflects the tools, materials, and vibe of a traditional luthier’s workspace.

One-Man Band Robot 3D Modeling

This project features a 3D model of a one-man band robot — a whimsical, multi-instrument-playing machine designed to perform music on its own. The goal was to combine mechanical elements with playful character design, capturing both function and personality.

Poster Design for Department Exhibition

This project involved designing a poster for a department exhibition showcasing student works. The poster was created to visually communicate the theme and atmosphere of the event while maintaining clarity and impact.

Poster Design Using Original Flower Photography

The poster was created using photos of flowers I took myself from around my neighborhood. I wanted to bring a natural and personal touch to the design by using original, organic visuals.

Running Animation Using Rotoscoping

For this project, I created an animation of a person running by using the rotoscoping technique. By tracing over live-action footage frame by frame, I aimed to capture realistic movement with a hand-drawn feel. This exercise helped me better understand motion and timing in animation.

Stop-Motion Animation with Action Figures

In this project, I created a stop-motion animation using action figures as characters. By carefully moving the figures frame by frame, I brought a small-scale story to life. This hands-on approach taught me patience and precision in animation production.
